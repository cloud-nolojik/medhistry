How it works

1
Receptionist registers patient
When a patient walks in, the receptionist adds them to the queue using our simple interface. No paperwork, no delays.
See Receptionist Portal →
2
Patient gives consent via OTP
Patient receives an SMS with a one-time code. They approve with a single tap. We fetch their medical history from ABDM and hospitals on file.
3
Patient appears in your queue
The patient shows up in your queue dashboard. Their briefing card is already being prepared.
